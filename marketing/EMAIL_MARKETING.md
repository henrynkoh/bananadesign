## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Tips
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use emojis sparingly
- Create urgency when appropriate
- Personalize with [First Name]
- A/B test different approaches

### Content Tips
- Keep it scannable (use headers, bullets, short paragraphs)
- Include clear CTAs
- Mobile-friendly design
- Personalize content
- Provide value in every email

### Timing
- Welcome series: Day 0, 1, 3, 7
- Re-engagement: 30, 60, 90 days inactive
- Promotional: Based on campaign schedule
- Educational: Weekly or bi-weekly

### Metrics to Track
- Open rate (aim for 20%+)
- Click-through rate (aim for 3%+)
- Conversion rate
- Unsubscribe rate (keep under 0.5%)
- Bounce rate
