## Best Practices for Instagram

1. **Visual Quality**: Use high-resolution, eye-catching images
2. **Mobile-First**: Optimize for mobile viewing
3. **Stories**: Use interactive stickers (polls, quizzes, links)
4. **Reels**: Follow trends, use popular music
5. **Hashtags**: Mix popular and niche hashtags
6. **Timing**: Post when your audience is most active
7. **Engagement**: Respond to comments quickly
8. **User-Generated Content**: Repost user designs with permission
9. **Influencer Partnerships**: Collaborate with design influencers
10. **Consistency**: Maintain brand aesthetic across all ads
