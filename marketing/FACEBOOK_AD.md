## Budget & Bidding Strategy

**Recommended Daily Budget:**
- Awareness: $50-100/day
- Conversion: $100-200/day
- Retargeting: $25-50/day

**Bidding Strategy:**
- Awareness: Lowest cost or cost per 1000 impressions
- Conversion: Cost per click or cost per conversion
- Retargeting: Cost per conversion (optimize for sign-ups)

**Recommended Placements:**

## A/B Testing Ideas

1. **Headlines**: Test different value propositions
2. **Images**: Test before/after vs. lifestyle images
3. **CTAs**: Test "Learn More" vs. "Get Started" vs. "Try Free"
4. **Text Length**: Test short vs. long copy
5. **Offers**: Test with discount vs. without

---

## Performance Metrics to Track

- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Cost per click (CPC)
- Cost per acquisition (CPA)
- Conversion rate
- Return on ad spend (ROAS)
- Engagement rate
- Video completion rate (if using video)

---

## Best Practices

1. **Use High-Quality Images**: Showcase the transformation clearly
2. **Clear Value Proposition**: State benefits immediately
3. **Strong CTA**: Make action obvious and easy
4. **Mobile-Optimized**: Most users are on mobile
5. **Test Regularly**: Continuously optimize based on data
6. **Retargeting**: Follow up with visitors who didn't convert
7. **Seasonal Relevance**: Adjust messaging for holidays/seasons
