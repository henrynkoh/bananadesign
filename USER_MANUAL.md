## Introduction

### Welcome to NanoBanana Pro

This manual will guide you through every aspect of using the application, from your first upload to advanced techniques for achieving professional results.

- **Homeowners**: Planning renovations or visualizing new spaces

- **DIY Enthusiasts**: Exploring design possibilities before committing to changes

## Uploading Floor Plans

### Supported File Formats

**Accepted Formats:**
- PNG (Portable Network Graphics) - Recommended for line drawings
- JPG/JPEG (Joint Photographic Experts Group) - Good for photos
- WEBP (Web Picture Format) - Modern, efficient format

**File Size Limits:**
- Minimum: 10KB
- Maximum: 10MB (recommended: 1-5MB)
- Optimal: 500KB - 2MB

### Preparing Your Floor Plan

#### Image Quality Guidelines

**Resolution:**
- Minimum: 800x600 pixels
- Recommended: 1920x1080 or higher
- Maximum: 4096x4096 pixels

**Clarity:**
- Use high-contrast images
- Ensure text and dimensions are readable
- Remove unnecessary watermarks if possible
- Clean, uncluttered floor plans work best

#### Content Recommendations

**What to Include:**
- Room boundaries and walls
- Room labels (bedroom, kitchen, etc.)
- Major fixtures (if shown)
- Doors and windows (if relevant)
- Scale or dimensions (helpful but not required)

**What to Avoid:**
- Excessive annotations or notes
- Multiple floor plans in one image
- Very low resolution or pixelated images
- Heavily compressed or distorted images

### Upload Methods

#### Method 1: Drag and Drop

1. Locate your floor plan file on your computer
2. Click and hold the file
3. Drag it over the upload area
4. Release when the area highlights
5. Wait for upload confirmation

**Visual Feedback:**
- Border changes color when dragging over
- Background may change color
- Cursor changes to indicate drop zone

#### Method 2: Click to Browse

1. Click anywhere in the upload area
2. File browser dialog opens
3. Navigate to your floor plan file
4. Select the file
5. Click "Open" or press Enter
6. Wait for upload confirmation

#### Method 3: Paste from Clipboard

1. Copy an image to your clipboard (Ctrl/Cmd + C)
2. Click in the upload area
3. Paste (Ctrl/Cmd + V)
4. Image uploads automatically

### After Upload

**What Happens:**
- Image preview appears immediately
- File is processed and optimized
- Ready for design generation
- Remove option becomes available

**Verification:**
- Check that the preview matches your file
- Ensure image is clear and readable
- Verify orientation is correct
- Confirm file size is reasonable

### Removing Uploaded Images

1. Hover over the uploaded image
2. Click the X button in the top-right corner
3. Confirm removal if prompted
4. Upload area returns to initial state

## Using the Design Assistant

### Understanding the Chat Interface

The Design Assistant is an intelligent chatbot that helps you describe your interior design vision. It understands natural language and can interpret various design preferences.

### Starting a Conversation

**Initial State:**
- Welcome message from the assistant
- Suggested prompt buttons displayed
- Input field ready for your description

**First Interaction:**
- You can click a suggested prompt
- Or type your own description
- Or combine both approaches

### Writing Effective Prompts

#### Basic Prompt Structure

A good prompt includes:
1. **Style**: Overall design aesthetic
2. **Colors**: Primary and accent colors
3. **Materials**: Flooring, countertops, etc.
4. **Furniture**: Types and styles
5. **Mood/Atmosphere**: Cozy, bright, luxurious, etc.

#### Example Prompts

**Simple:**
- "Modern minimalist"
- "Cozy Scandinavian"
- "Luxury contemporary"

**Detailed:**
- "Modern minimalist style with white walls, light oak floors, black metal accents, and lots of natural light. Include a sleek kitchen with marble countertops and minimalist furniture."
- "Cozy Scandinavian design with white walls, light wood floors, soft textiles, green plants, and warm lighting. Include a reading nook with a comfortable chair."

**Very Detailed:**
- "Luxury contemporary interior with marble floors, gold fixtures, velvet furniture in deep blues and grays, floor-to-ceiling windows, modern art pieces, and sophisticated lighting. Include a statement chandelier and high-end appliances."

### Using Suggested Prompts

**Benefits:**
- Quick start for common styles
- Learn effective prompt structure
- Consistent terminology
- Proven to work well

**How to Use:**
1. Review available suggestions
2. Click one that matches your vision
3. Modify it if needed
4. Or use as-is for quick generation

**Customizing Suggestions:**
- Click a suggestion to load it
- Edit the text before sending
- Combine multiple suggestions
- Add your own details

### Advanced Prompt Techniques

#### Style Combinations

You can combine multiple styles:
- "Modern Scandinavian with industrial accents"
- "Traditional with contemporary elements"
- "Minimalist luxury"

#### Specific Room Focus

Mention specific rooms:
- "Focus on the kitchen with modern appliances"
- "Make the living room cozy and inviting"
- "Design a luxurious master bedroom"

#### Color Schemes

Be specific about colors:
- "Neutral palette with navy blue accents"
- "Warm earth tones with cream and beige"
- "Bold colors: emerald green and gold"

#### Lighting Descriptions

Describe lighting preferences:
- "Bright and airy with lots of natural light"
- "Warm, cozy lighting with table lamps"
- "Dramatic lighting with spotlights"

### Sending Your Prompt

**Methods:**
1. Click the Send button
2. Press Enter (without Shift)
3. Click outside the input (if configured)

**After Sending:**
- Message appears in chat
- Assistant acknowledges
- Generation begins
- Progress indicator shows

### Chat History

**Viewing History:**
- Scroll up to see previous messages
- All conversations are visible
- Can reference earlier prompts

**Starting Fresh:**
- Upload a new floor plan
- Or continue with current one
- Previous messages remain for reference

## Troubleshooting

### Upload Issues

**Problem: File Won't Upload**

**Solutions:**
- Check file format (PNG, JPG, JPEG, WEBP only)
- Verify file size (under 10MB)
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache
- Check internet connection

**Problem: Upload Takes Too Long**

**Solutions:**
- Check file size (reduce if very large)
- Verify internet speed
- Try compressing the image
- Check for browser extensions interfering

**Problem: Image Preview Looks Wrong**

**Solutions:**
- Verify original file is correct
- Try re-uploading
- Check image isn't corrupted
- Ensure proper file format

### Generation Issues

**Problem: Generation Fails**

**Solutions:**
- Check internet connection
- Verify API token is valid
- Check Replicate account has credits
- Try a simpler prompt
- Reduce image size

**Problem: Generation Takes Too Long**

**Solutions:**
- This is normal (30-60 seconds)
- Check internet speed
- Don't close browser tab
- Be patient, complex designs take time
- Check Replicate service status

**Problem: Results Don't Match Prompt**

**Solutions:**
- Be more specific in prompt
- Try different wording
- Use style names explicitly
- Mention colors and materials
- Generate multiple times

### Display Issues

**Problem: Images Don't Show**

**Solutions:**
- Refresh the page
- Check browser console for errors
- Try different browser
- Clear browser cache
- Check internet connection

**Problem: Download Doesn't Work**

**Solutions:**
- Check browser download settings
- Verify pop-up blocker isn't interfering
- Try right-click and "Save As"
- Check available disk space
- Try different browser

### Performance Issues

**Problem: Application Is Slow**

**Solutions:**
- Close other browser tabs
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Update browser to latest version
- Disable browser extensions

**Problem: Browser Crashes**

**Solutions:**
- Update browser
- Clear cache and cookies
- Disable extensions
- Check available RAM
- Try different browser

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is NanoBanana Pro free to use?**
A: The application itself is open-source and free. However, AI generation requires API credits from Replicate, which offers a free tier with limited credits. Additional usage may require paid credits.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: It depends on the deployment. Some versions may require accounts for usage tracking, while others work without registration.

**Q: Can I use this commercially?**
A: Check the license terms. Generally, generated images can be used for commercial purposes, but verify the AI model's terms of service.

**Q: How accurate are the results?**
A: Results are impressive visualizations but may not be architecturally precise. They're best used for inspiration and concept visualization rather than construction blueprints.

**Q: Can I edit the generated designs?**
A: Yes! Download the images and edit them in any image editing software like Photoshop, GIMP, or Canva.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What image formats are supported?**
A: PNG, JPG, JPEG, and WEBP formats are supported for uploads.

**Q: What's the maximum file size?**
A: Recommended maximum is 10MB, though smaller files (1-5MB) work best.

**Q: How long does generation take?**
A: Typically 30-60 seconds, depending on complexity and server load.

**Q: Can I generate multiple designs at once?**
A: Currently, designs are generated one at a time, but you can generate multiple variations sequentially.

**Q: Do I need special software?**
A: No, NanoBanana Pro runs entirely in your web browser. No downloads or installations required (except for self-hosting).

### Design Questions

**Q: What design styles are supported?**
A: Virtually any style can be described. Popular styles include Modern, Minimalist, Scandinavian, Traditional, Industrial, Luxury, and Contemporary.

**Q: Can I specify exact furniture?**
A: You can describe furniture types and styles, but exact brand matches aren't guaranteed. Be descriptive about what you want.

**Q: How do I get better results?**
A: Use high-quality floor plans, write detailed prompts, be specific about colors and materials, and don't hesitate to iterate multiple times.

**Q: Can I control room-specific designs?**
A: Yes, mention specific rooms in your prompt (e.g., "Focus on the kitchen with modern appliances").

**Q: What if I don't like the result?**
A: Try refining your prompt with more specific details, or generate again - AI results can vary between generations.

### Privacy and Security

**Q: Are my floor plans stored?**
A: Check the privacy policy. Typically, images are processed and not permanently stored, but verify for your specific deployment.

**Q: Who can see my designs?**
A: Designs are private to your session unless you explicitly share them.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: The application uses secure connections (HTTPS) and follows best practices, but review the security policy for your deployment.
